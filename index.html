David Okeyode
Certified Instructor and Principal Consultant
David is an independent Azure Security practitioner helping companies to secure their Azure environments through private expert level trainings and assessments. He has authored two books on Azure security – “Penetration Testing Azure for Ethical Hackers” and “Microsoft Azure Security Technologies Certification and Beyond” (https://amzn.to/3C7mrcL). He has also authored multiple cloud computing courses for the popular cybersecurity training platform – Cybrary.
